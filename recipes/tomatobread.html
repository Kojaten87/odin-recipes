Tomato Bread

This is a recipe for a tasty tomato bread

Its very easy to do recipe.

Ingridients

  • bread
  • butter
  • tomatoes
  • onions

Steps

  1. Take the bread and put butter on it.
  2. Slice the tomatos into slices and the onion into cubes.
  3. put the tomato slices on the bread and top it with the onions
