Lasagna

This is a recipe for a tasty lasagna

Its very easy to do recipe.

Ingridients

  • lasagna plates
  • meat sauce
  • bechamel sauce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Take the sauce and put one layer into the glass bowl.
  2. Put a layer of sauce into it, put a layer of the bechamel on top and then continue like this.
  3. Finish with a layer of bechamel and put the cheese on top.
